Gameplay

In game, you are dropped from orbit onto alien worlds in order to beat back the enemies of democracy, and spread the rule of righteous liberation across the galaxy. The game's story progresses in real time, shifting with the planets that fall to the enemies of liberty.

THE TERMINID SWARM

The Terminids a vile race of insectoid creatures who spread through a galactic spore cloud known as "The Gloom". These creatures produced a substance known as E-710, that fuels the starships of Super Earth.

THE AUTOMATON MENACE

Starting as the cybernetically-enhanced workers of Cyberstan, who rebelled during the first Galactic War, these dissidents returned to the galaxy with a legion of automated soldiers programmed with one purpose: to destroy Super Earth

THE ILLUMINATE CULT

originally a peaceful race of aliens, the Illuminate were driven from the galaxy after the discovery of Illuminate WMDs (Weapons of Mass Destruction), they returned, looking to crush Super Earth underneath the armored boot of Tyranny
